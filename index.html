Project Map: nosmalltalkKL

Skip the small talk. Meet your people.

A social matching app for young professionals in Kuala Lumpur

What is this?

nosmalltalkKL is an app where young professionals in Kuala Lumpur take a short personality quiz, get matched with compatible people, and then meet them across 4 weekends — starting with big social events and gradually narrowing down to an intimate dinner and finally a one-on-one blind date.

The app also automatically promotes itself through social media, WhatsApp, and email, books venues across KL, and sends invitations and reminders to everyone involved — so the whole experience runs smoothly without manual work.
